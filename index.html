scoring logicformula + methodology

The ecosystem score combines account health, audience sentiment, and cross-platform momentum into one unified creator signal.

  • Account score measures content clarity, engagement, and relative platform growth.
  • Audience signal measures emotional intelligence, trust resonance, and quote-level reaction.
  • Momentum measures growth rate, share potential, and consistency across platforms.
